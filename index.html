Overview

The F-35 Lightning II is a family of single-seat, single-engine, all-weather stealth multirole combat aircraft. Designed to perform ground-attack and air-superiority missions, it represents a leap in situational awareness, survivability, and versatility compared to previous generations of fighters.

Variants

  • F-35A: Conventional takeoff and landing (CTOL) variant for traditional runways.
  • F-35B: Short takeoff / vertical landing (STOVL) variant for amphibious assault ships and short airstrips.
  • F-35C: Carrier variant (CV) with larger wings and reinforced landing gear for aircraft carrier operations.

Key Features

  • Advanced stealth design for reduced radar cross-section.
  • Sensor fusion for enhanced pilot situational awareness.
  • Supersonic performance with efficient fuel usage.
  • Ability to share targeting data with allied forces in real-time.
